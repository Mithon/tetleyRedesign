This is simply a guide! Feel free to tweak your variables to get the perfect cup of Tea for you!

Water Temps

Not every type of Tetley Tea likes to be brewed at the same temperature! Follow this guideline to produce the optimal taste!

Green Tea: 174°F
Black Tea: 200°F
Red Tea: 208°F
Herbal Tea: 208°F
