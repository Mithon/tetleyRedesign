intro sentence

Here at Tetley Canada, we pride ourselves on the quality of our teas. We have over 45 vibrant blends of black, green, red and herbal teas, to meet your needs - helping you to live a better everyday.

The story of tea is as old as nature itself. But we like to think it took its most exciting turn in 1837 when the Tetley brothers went into business in Yorkshire, England.

We are always working to make sure our tea improves the lives of the people who grow and pick it, just as much as the people who drink it. Only by respecting the environment and protecting the livelihoods of people working on the tea gardens can we be assured that our supply chains have a long term future.
